Dejarik

Dejarik, also known as Holochess, is a game featured in Star Wars, dating all the way back to A New Hope. Although there was no complete ruleset present in the movies, two official rulesets have been created, one for the Jedi Challenges video game and one for Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge in Disneyland. Additionally, about a dozen fan rulesets have been created, some of which have their own variations. However, few are easily playable without a physical Dejarik set. This website attempts to make them playable.

Blake Whitlow's document is an invaluable resource for studying Dejarik variants and formed the basis for this website.
